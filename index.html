Welcome to GTHS Mamfe

Explore our technical programs and vibrant student life.

Industrial Department

Power System Engineering

Hands-on training in electrical power systems, generation, transmission, and industrial installations. Students gain practical skills in high-voltage operations, circuit analysis, and safety standards for working in electrical environments.

Building Construction

Develop practical skills in architectural drawing, structural design, masonry, and construction planning. Emphasis is placed on modern building techniques, materials, and sustainability.

Carpentry & Joinery

Master the craft of woodworking, furniture design, and structural joinery. Students are trained in using traditional and modern tools to construct quality wood-based products for industrial and domestic use.

Commercial Department

Office Automation

Learn to use digital office tools, manage data, and enhance productivity through automation. This course prepares students for the modern workplace with skills in spreadsheets, word processing, and administrative software.

Secretarial Studies

Train in document processing, office management, shorthand, and effective communication. Students become proficient in organizing meetings, managing schedules, and performing secretarial duties with professionalism.

Accounting

Gain solid foundations in bookkeeping, financial reporting, taxation, and business mathematics. This program equips students with the skills to manage business finances and prepare for careers in finance or entrepreneurship.

About GTHS Mamfe

GTHS Mamfe was created in 1980 by a presidential decree. Since its establishment, the school has nurtured thousands of students through dedicated administrators and qualified staff. With departments in both industrial and commercial education, GTHS Mamfe continues to lead as the top technical high school in the division, producing competent graduates for both national and international opportunities.
