Welcome to GTHS Mamfe

Explore our technical programs and vibrant student life.

Government Technical High School (GTHS) Mamfe is a renowned technical institution located in the South West Region of Cameroon. It plays a vital role in equipping students with both academic knowledge and hands-on skills necessary for the job market and further studies. The school is structured into two main sections: the Industrial Department and the Commercial Department, each comprising a First Cycle and a Second Cycle. The Industrial Department offers specialized training in various fields, including Electrical Power Engineering, Building and Construction, Wood Works, Home Economics, and Fashion Designing. These programs are designed to provide students with practical and technical expertise for careers in industry and craftsmanship. The Commercial Department focuses on business and administrative skills, with programs in Accounting, Office Automation, and Secretarial Studies. This section prepares students for roles in the business sector, emphasizing both theoretical and practical business knowledge. GTHS Mamfe remains committed to academic excellence and vocational training, fostering a new generation of skilled professionals in Cameroon.
