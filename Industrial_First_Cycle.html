First Cycle Industrial Department GTHS Mamfe

This department is opened to both male and female students. In most cases, female students prefer Electrical Power Engineering. There are three sections in this department: Electrical Power Engineering, Building and constructionand Wood Works. The students move through an orientation where they have an opportunity to study all the deciplines. At the end of the orientation process, the students freely choose that they intend to study in the second year of their studies. At the end of the four study period, there students have an exam to write organised by GCE BOARD. The students study both general education and technical related subjuects.
